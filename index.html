Technology & Operations

Plant

Containerised slow-pyrolysis with emissions abatement, automated feed, and waste-heat recovery for drying. Designed to meet EBC Quality and C-Sink thresholds (e.g., H/Corg < 0.7).

Feedstock

Local Grade A/B wood waste, estate thinnings, on-farm hedgerow arisings, plus herbaceous blends (straw/leys) with moisture control and screening for stable, certifiable char.

Sinks & MRV

Soil sinks on-farm and with neighbours, plus material sinks documented with GPS, batch logs and independent labs; issuance via Puro.earth / Carbonfuture.

Roadmap (0–24 months)

  • 0–2 months: Lock feedstock MOUs (arborists, estates, straw); engage MRV partner; EA pre-app.
  • 3–6 months: Planning & permit submission; OEM shortlist & diligence; finance close (grants/green loan/partner).
  • 7–12 months: Civils & utilities; covered bays; equipment delivery & installation; commissioning & trial batches.
  • 13–24 months: EBC audit; MRV onboarding; first issuance & sales; ramp to nameplate; scale (second line / heat use / material sinks).

Risks & Mitigations

  • Feedstock shortfall/quality: Multi-year contracts; moisture/size specs; on-site pre-drying; QA sampling.
  • Price volatility: Forward offtakes; diversified sinks; optional biochar/heat sales.
  • Permit delays: EA pre-app; experienced consultant; phased feedstock list.
  • Equipment downtime: OEM service plan; critical spares; trained operator coverage.
  • MRV non-compliance: EBC pre-audit; regimented sampling; data logs; platform QA.
