The Opportunity

Why now

Durable removals are supply-constrained. Biochar leads delivered CDR volumes and commands premium pricing with credible MRV. EU/UK policy tailwinds are reducing buyer risk.

Why here

On-farm siting in Oxfordshire within a tight logistics radius. Trusted land stewardship, access to clean woody feedstocks, and diversified soil/material sinks enable defensible, scalable supply.

Technology & Operations

Plant

Containerised slow-pyrolysis with emissions abatement, automated feed, and waste-heat recovery for drying. Designed to meet EBC Quality and C-Sink thresholds (e.g., H/Corg < 0.7).

Feedstock

Local Grade A/B wood waste, estate thinnings, on-farm hedgerow arisings; herbaceous blends (straw/leys) as appropriate with moisture control and screening for stable, certifiable char.

Sinks & MRV

Soil sinks on-farm and with neighbours, plus material sinks documented with GPS, batch logs, and independent labs; issuance via Puro.earth / Carbonfuture.

Market & Customers

Target corporates with 2030/2050 net-zero commitments requiring high-integrity, durable removals. Priority segments: UK/EU technology and professional services, heavy industry residuals, and climate-forward consumer brands. GTM blends direct relationships, platform listings, and forward offtake MoUs.
